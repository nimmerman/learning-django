Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) strives to help progress the engineering student body through leadership, scholarship, and service. Due to the disciplines that the EECS department encompasses, the Beta Epsilon chapter here at the University of Michigan is composed of electrical, computer, and computer science engineers. As an organization, we bring services to the greater student body and community through the dB Cafe and other services, and we help our own members by providing networking and educational opportunities.
